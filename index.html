About Healing Source

Healing source is a trusted Medical Tourism Company based out of Bengaluru(India) and Muscat(Oman) helping patients who are looking for reliable and trustworthy medical treatment in India especially in the area of cancer. With a proven track record, we value your time and your health equally and strongly believe in providing personal attention to each of our patients.

We provided end to end solutions to ensure you receive the best possible treatment. We help you plan your travel, choose the right accommodation, doctors, hospitals and all necessary prerequisites to ensure that your pre and post treatment is successful and you have a pleasant stay in India.

Have a look at our process and what our customers say below.

Planning

We provide a varied and extensive list of hospitals and destinations to choose from for our patients. We provide tailored advice to each of our patients the best options for hospitals and doctors based on the type of treatment required and your preferred destinations. In addition to this, we ensure that every patient is looked after with utmost care and special attention is offered for the differently-abled, along with arrangement for transportation, hospitality, food, visa, tickets and travel and tourism.

Organizing

We understand what goes on in the mind of a patient and the family when it comes to making decisions regarding staying and treatment in a different country. This is why, we make sure that all your needs are taken of right from invitation letters for embassies as application for medical visa, assistance in booking flights to take you to your destination, include a prior session, a consultation with the doctor if and when required, schedule appointments with hospitals of your choice, assign a dedicated program manager for you, and even an interpreter or communication aid if necessary

Successful Treatment

We ensure that a great importance is given to your treatment and stay. We work with you at all times right from arranging your travel tickets, ensuring you get your medical visas, booking your accommodation and travel, consultation with your doctor, follows up check-ups, recreational and relaxing sight-seeing and shopping tours for recuperating patients post the treatment. We want our patients to have a successful treatment and a great stay so we go an extra mile to ensure this.
